Campus Maps

Camosun has two campuses: Historical Lansdowne campus sits high on the corner of Lansdowne and Foul Bay Roads, offering spectacular clear-day views of Victoria and the Olympic Mountains. Interurban campus rests in a rural Saanich setting, only a short 15-minute drive from downtown.
